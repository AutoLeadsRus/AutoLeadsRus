GDPR Compliance Statement

Last Updated: January 2024

AutoLeadsRus is fully committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

Our GDPR Commitments

  • Lawful Processing: We only process data when we have a legal basis to do so
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the data we need to provide our services
  • Security Measures: Implementation of appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • Data Subject Rights: We facilitate all GDPR rights requests

Data Processing Addendum

We offer a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) for customers who require it under GDPR Article 28.

International Transfers

When transferring EU data internationally, we use Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved mechanisms.
