CCPA/CPRA Compliance

Last Updated: January 2024

AutoLeadsRus complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the CPRA.

California Consumer Rights

Under CCPA/CPRA, California residents have:

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Of sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to Correct: Inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Limit: Use of sensitive personal information

How to Exercise Your Rights

California residents may submit requests through:

  1. Our online portal at www.autoleadsrus.com/ccpa-request
  2. Email to privacy@autoleadsrus.com
  3. Toll-free number: 1-800-AUTO-LEAD
